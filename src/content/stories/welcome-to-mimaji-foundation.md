Today we officially launch MiMaji Foundation — the social impact arm of [MiMaji](https://mimaji.co.ke), Kenya's water delivery technology platform.

## Why We Started

While building MiMaji as a water delivery platform in Nairobi, we discovered something troubling: the communities that needed clean water most were the ones paying the highest prices for the worst quality. Residents in informal settlements like Kibera pay up to 10 times more per litre than those in affluent neighbourhoods like Karen.

This isn't just a market failure — it's a human rights issue.

## What We're Building

MiMaji Foundation focuses on three pillars:

1. **Water Quality Testing** — bringing free testing to communities so residents know if their water is safe
2. **Community Education** — teaching safe water practices through schools and community health workers
3. **Data & Advocacy** — building [MajiMap](/majimap/), an interactive platform that makes water inequality visible and actionable

## The MiMaji Connection

Every water order placed through [MiMaji.co.ke](https://mimaji.co.ke) supports our foundation's work. The commercial platform and the foundation work hand in hand — technology powering impact.
